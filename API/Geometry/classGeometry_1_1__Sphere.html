Detailed Description

template<typename _T>
class Geometry::_Sphere< _T >

Representation of a sphere in three-dimensional space. The sphere is stored as the three-dimensional position of its center and its radius.

Convert a position on the unit sphere given in spherical coordinates into Cartesian coordinates. The unit sphere is centered at the origin.

Parameters
inclinationInclination angle from [0, PI]
azimuthAzimuth angle from [0, 2 * PI)
Returns
Three-dimensional vector representing a position on the unit sphere
Note
An inclination of zero corresponds to the north pole (0, 1, 0), independent of the azimuth.
An inclination of PI corresponds to the south pole (0, -1, 0), independent of the azimuth.
An inclination of PI/2 and azimuth of zero corresponds to the vector (1, 0, 0).
An inclination of PI/2 and azimuth of 1/2 * PI corresponds to the vector (0, 0, 1).
An inclination of PI/2 and azimuth of PI corresponds to the vector (-1, 0, 0).
An inclination of PI/2 and azimuth of 3/2 * PI corresponds to the vector (0, 0, -1).
